# Developer Charter for Python-3 Virtual Core 2025

## 1. Purpose
This Developer Charter outlines the guidelines and responsibilities for developers contributing to the Python-3 Virtual Core 2025 project, ensuring adherence to copyright regulations as per the Intellectual Property Department of Hong Kong, China.

## 2. Developer Rights
- **Contribution**: Developers have the right to contribute code, documentation, and other materials to the project.
- **Attribution**: Contributors will receive appropriate credit for their contributions.

## 3. Developer Responsibilities
- **Respect Copyright**: Developers must ensure that their contributions do not infringe on the copyrights of others. Original work is encouraged.
- **No Commercial Use**: Contributions must not be made for commercial purposes without explicit permission.
- **Documentation**: Developers must provide clear documentation for their contributions, including usage instructions and licensing information.

## 4. Code of Conduct
- **Collaboration**: Developers are expected to collaborate respectfully and constructively with others.
- **Transparency**: Developers should maintain transparency in their contributions and communicate any potential conflicts of interest.

## 5. Limitations
- The project is provided "as is," and developers assume all risks associated with their contributions.
- The project maintainers are not liable for any issues arising from the use of the code or contributions.
